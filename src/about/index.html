Who We Are

Kemsan Acres Global LTD, a reputable Nigerian real estate company, specializes in land procurement, real estate development, and architectural design. Registered with the Real Estate Developers Association of Nigeria, we aim to make property ownership accessible to all.

Founded in 2019 and headquartered in Ibadan, we operate across multiple states, including Lagos, Ogun, Ondo, Oyo, Ekiti, and FCT Abuja, with estates in each location.

Our team ensures you get the property you pay for through guided inspections and 24/7 customer support to address any concerns.
